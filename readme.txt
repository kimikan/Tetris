Usage:
AVK_UP : Change Shape
AVK_DOWN: Speed Down
AVK_LEFT: Left
AVK_RIGHT: Right.

AVK_SOFT2: Quit
AVK_CLR: Start/Stop
AVK_ENTER: Pause/Start
